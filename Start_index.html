What We Do

We are a non-profit that strives to spread a wave of education reform. We believe in equitable education for all students, regardless of their economic and social status.


There are talented students all around that do not have the resources they need to excel, which only limits the growth of our society. By helping these students reach their maximum potential, we can increase economic mobility and societal growth.
